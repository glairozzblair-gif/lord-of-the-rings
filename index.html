The Hobbit & Lord of the Rings

Welcome to the world of Middle-earth, where epic adventures unfold!

Books

The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1937

A fantasy novel that follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit who embarks on an unexpected adventure.

The Desolation of Smaug

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1937

The second part of Bilbo Baggins' adventure, where he and the company of dwarves face the dragon Smaug.

The Battle of the Five Armies

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1937

The thrilling conclusion to The Hobbit, featuring a massive battle for control of the Lonely Mountain's treasure.

The Fellowship of the Ring

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1954

The first volume of The Lord of the Rings, detailing the formation of the Fellowship and their quest to destroy the One Ring.

The Two Towers

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1954

The second volume of The Lord of the Rings, focusing on the split of the Fellowship and their individual journeys.

The Return of the King

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Published: 1955

The final volume of The Lord of the Rings, concluding the epic tale with the defeat of Sauron and the restoration of peace to Middle-earth.
